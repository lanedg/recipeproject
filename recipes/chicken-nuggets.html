Ingredients

  • Frozen Chicken Nuggets

Steps

  1. Place frozen nuggets on a plate
  2. Put the plate inside the microwave
  3. Heat for 30 seconds
  4. Flip over each nugget and heat for 30 seconds
  5. Take the plate out of the microwave
  6. Let cool for one minute
